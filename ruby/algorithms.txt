Locate desired floor number and current floor number using elevator directory. 
If desired floor number is greater than current floor number press up
 	-Wait for elevator doors to open 
	 -Enter the elevator
Else if desired floor number is lower than current floor number press down
	-Wait for elevator doors to open
	-Enter the elevator
Else you are on the correct floor, no need to use an elevator

After entering the elevator 	
	-Press desired floor number
 	-Watch the elevator view screen to track your current floor
Until the elevator view screen shows your desired number
 	-Stay on Elevator 
When the view screen displays your desired floor number
	-Wait for doors to open
	-Exit Elevator
